Drawing inspiration from classic Americana, The Original reinvents the neighborhood eatery to create a timeless fusion of old and new, blending familiar comforts with an inventive, playful modern flair.

About Cuisines

It is an absolute art !

Our creative, elevated food and beverage program combines satisfying staples with imaginative twists. From boozy brunches and happy hours to family dinners, special occasions and everything in between, The Original has something for everyone.

Our menus feature familiar, elevated comforts including a variety of gourmet sandwiches, soups and all-day breakfast options alongside enticing entrées like our Prime Rib Dinner, Short Rib Stroganoff and other thoughtfully sourced proteins.
Unique and craveable curiosities include our signature Croughnut Slider Burger, a quirky collection of boozy milkshakes and a pie case offering a rotating selection of sweet treats.
